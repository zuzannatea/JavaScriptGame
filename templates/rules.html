Rules of the Game

  • Upgrade your character through stat boosts and advancing extra moves


How to Play

  • To upgrade character through stat boosts, collect brightly-coloured stat boosts
  • To unlock extra combat moves, progress to the next level
  • To progress to the next level, increase your score
  • To increase your score, defeat enemies


Enemies


Zombie

  • It will always track you, regardless of where you are on the map

Swarmer

  • If they’re alone, they will seek to find a friend.
  • If they have friends but less than 3 of them, they will wander.
  • If they have more than 3 friends, they will hunt you.
  • Be careful! When in a swarm, they get a speed boost.

Charger

  • If there exists a straight, unobstructed path between it and you, it will gain a speed boost and begin rushing straight at you.
  • Note: once it spots a straight path, a line will appear, warning you of it.

Splitter

  • It has three lives. If you kill it, it will respawn into two smaller versions of itself, each one having two lives of their own.
  • Note: the smaller, spawned versions of Splitters have less health.

Teleporter

  • If it detects player is close enough, it will teleport to player’s surroundings and wait a small cooldown.
  • Teleporting has a cooldown as well; if it’s active, Teleporter will simply hunt player manually.
  • Note: right before teleporting, the tile the Teleporter will appear on is marked.


Extra Moves

There are three extra moves. Each one can be first gained, and then upgraded upon completing a level.

  • Smash causes damage to all entities in the player’s surroundings.
    • It has three tiers: Smash (regular ranged attack), Double Smash (extra ranged attack) and Mega Smash (an even more extra ranged attack and several seconds of invulnerability.)

  • Roll lets the player move while becoming invincible for a short moment.
    • It has three tiers: Roll (a very short invulnerability period), Prolonged Roll (a slightly longer invulnerability period) and Damage Roll (an even longer invulnerability period, plus causes damage to all entities in the player’s surroundings.)

  • Charge lets the player attack the enemies with a speed boost.
    • It has three tiers: Charge (a slight increase in player speed for a moment), Faster Charge (the player’s speed is doubled for a longer moment) and Explosiong Charge (an even bigger increase in player speed for longer, plus a ranged attack.)


Extras


Cheats

It’s possible to turn on invulnerability, kill aura (player automatically attacks all enemies without keyboard input) and give the player extra move boosts


Settings

It’s possible to change keybinds and turn the music on and off
